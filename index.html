Website Audit for yourfora.com

What We Found

We examined yourfora.com to understand why qualified outpatient practices aren't converting into assessment calls. Here's what the data shows.

Authority Gap

Site doesn't acknowledge accounts receivable crossing 30%, denials at 20-25%, billing staff quitting, or payer audits. No specialty-specific language (DSM-5, KX modifiers, evaluation and management coding). Generic positioning identical to competitors.

Conversion Leak

Generic "Connect with us" CTA doesn't pre-qualify or create urgency. Manual scheduling process loses 50% of interested practices between inquiry and call. Average 5-7 day lag to book.

Discovery Problem

Missing specialty pages (Behavioral Health, Physical Therapy, Orthopedics, Urgent Care). Missing problem pages (denials, accounts receivable aging). Missing local pages (Washington DC, Maryland, Virginia). Can't rank for crisis-mode searches. Invisible in AI search results.

Distribution Absence

No content to distribute. No videos, blog, case studies. No LinkedIn or YouTube presence. No email nurture sequences. Visitor comes once, leaves, never returns.
